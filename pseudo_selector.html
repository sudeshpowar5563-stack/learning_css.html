Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj was a visionary warrior king who founded the Maratha Empire in 17th-century India, born on February 19, 1630, at Shivneri Fort. He is revered for his military prowess, administrative reforms, and efforts to establish "Swarajya" or self-rule. Shivaji was known for his innovative use of guerrilla warfare, his strong navy, and his commitment to protecting his subjects, which included promoting agriculture, trade, and justice for all.
